My story

I know what it feels like to be stuck.

To try hard and see no change. To feel like your body doesn't reflect who you are.

That's exactly why I coach. I used to drink most weekends just to feel something outside of work. Then everything changed — I found lifting, found a plan, and eventually found myself on a PCA stage as a pro athlete.

Because I've lived every stage of this story, I coach people who are living the early chapters of it: professionals with full diaries, parents running on five hours' sleep, people who eat "healthily" and can't work out why nothing moves.
